About Me.

Software Developer & products Designer

I’m Andrew, but people call me “Alah”. I care deeply about building products that are usable and pleasant for it users. My professional life has been 100% driven by my passion for design and structure. I was sketching crazy detailed 3d models for fun when i graduated from secondary school, started building promo websites for small businesses while in school, then developed an app for my gaming guild in my secondary school days. For 2 years, I’ve worked for an e-commerce platform in Jos, where I was able to drive a 9.8% growth in sales by shaving five seconds from the website’s loading time. I was in charge of implementing and launching the mobile app for a small Startup in Yola, which I was able to complete in just under three months. Not that learning React Native at the same time made things easy for me. Still, in the end, I was able to deliver an app that’s used by hundreds of distributors and direct customers daily and did so on-time and on-budget. Right now, I’m excited about the still very complicated WebAssembly, and working towards becoming a React senior. In the following years, I also plan to explore the “server-side” more and become a better-rounded full-stack dev.
